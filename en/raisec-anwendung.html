Optimally Use Your RAISEC Results

A 5-step plan for practical application

1

Self-Reflection

Analyze your RAISEC results and consider which aspects surprise or confirm your expectations.

Reflection Questions:

  • Which results surprise you?
  • Which match your self-assessment?
  • Where do you see development potential?
2

Career Mapping

Identify career fields and positions that match your RAISEC profile.

Career Planning:

  • Research suitable career fields
  • Identify skill gaps
  • Define career goals
  • Create development plan
3

Skill Development

Systematically develop the skills that match your RAISEC profile.

Development Areas:

  • Hard skills for your profile
  • Strengthen soft skills
  • Certificates and training
  • Gain practical experience
4

Networking & Mentoring

Build a network with people who have similar RAISEC profiles.

Networking Strategies:

  • Attend industry events
  • Join online communities
  • Find mentors
  • Form peer groups
5

Continuous Adaptation

Regularly review your RAISEC profile and adjust your strategy.

Adaptation Strategies:

  • Annual RAISEC retake
  • Review career goals
  • Consider new trends
  • Develop flexibility

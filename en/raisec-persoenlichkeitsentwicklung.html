Understanding the RAISEC Model

A proven tool for career orientation and personality analysis

Scientific Background

The RAISEC model (also called Holland Codes) was developed by John L. Holland and is one of the best-validated tools for career orientation. Over 50 years of research and millions of participants have confirmed its effectiveness.

What is the RAISEC Model?

The RAISEC model (also called Holland Codes) was developed by John L. Holland and is one of the most well-known and frequently used tools for career orientation. It is based on the assumption that people and careers can be divided into six basic personality types.

R - Realistic

Core Personality: Practical, hands-on, technically oriented

Strengths: Problem-solving, manual skills, technical understanding

Values: Honesty, directness, practical results

Interest Areas:
Machines & Technology Crafts Physical Work Nature & Environment
Suitable Careers:
  • Engineer (Mechanical, Electrical)
  • Technician & Mechanic
  • Farmer & Forester
  • Police & Security
  • Craftsman (Carpenter, Electrician)

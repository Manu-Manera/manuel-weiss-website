Why Ikigai Matters

  • Life Satisfaction: People with a clear Ikigai are more satisfied and happier
  • Longevity: In Okinawa, where Ikigai originated, people live particularly long lives
  • Motivation: A clear Ikigai gives you a reason to get up every day
  • Decision Making: Helps with important life decisions
  • Stress Reduction: Reduces stress through clear life goals
